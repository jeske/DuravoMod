Smart Minion Pathfinding

Your minions now intelligently navigate around terrain. When blocked by obstacles, they find paths around them instead of getting stuck. When truly isolated, they phase back to you to prevent cheese strategies.

Persistent Position

Your character stays exactly where you left them when you log out. No more spawning at the world origin. The only fast travel is pylons — making your journey through Terraria feel truly connected.

Screenshot: Player logging back in at exact position in underground jungle, with spawn immunity shield visible

Emergency Shield System

Early armor now provides reactive shields that absorb incoming damage. Copper/Tin gives a flat 30 HP shield, while Gold/Platinum provides 15% of your max HP and purges debuffs. Visual feedback helps new players learn combat timing.

Screenshot: Player being hit with cyan shield particles absorbing damage, showing "(blocked)" combat text

Shiny Set Bonus

Complete armor sets now grant "Shiny" — nearby ores and gems sparkle with particle effects, making them easier to spot while mining. Breaking your set for better pieces no longer means losing critical combat bonuses.

Screenshot: Underground scene with sparkling ore veins highlighted by the Shiny buff particles
